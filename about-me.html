Evan Peterson - About Me

Evan Peterson is a Sophomore at Purdue University pursuing a bachelor’s degree in UX Design.

He aspires to better existing technology and make it more accessible to a larger variety of people, such as the older generations or those less experienced with technology, as well as create original designs for new and emerging technology. He is experienced in design tools such as Photoshop, Gimp, Figma, and Canva as well as in 3D CAD programs such as Blender, Tinkercad, and Fusion 360. He has experience with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. He plans on using his skills to make technology a better and easier experience for all users so that it can become more accessible in the future. (Image courtesy of Nancy Rasche)

He is also pursuing a Certificate in Acting allowing him to continue his passion for theatre while he attends college. He has been in many shows such as "Little Shop of Horrors" and "12 Angry Jurrors" and was an understudy in Purdue's production of "A Midsummer Night's Dream".

He also has a passion for 3D printing and is a member and supervisor of the 3D Printing Club at Purdue University. He enjoys printing and finishing helmets and other props from movies, has printed and finished existing as well as his own original models, and has even printed props for theatrical productions.
