Evan Peterson - About Me

I am a Sophomore at Purdue University pursuing a bachelor’s degree in UX Design.

I aspires to better existing technology and make it more accessible to a larger variety of people, such as the older generations or those less experienced with technology, as well as create original designs for new and emerging technology. I am experienced in design tools such as Figma, Canva, Photoshop, and Gimp as well as in 3D CAD programs such as Blender, Tinkercad, and Fusion 360. I have experience with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Javascript. I favor hands on work including prototyping and conducting user interviews. I plan on using these skills to make technology a better and easier experience for all users so that it can become more accessible in the future. (Image courtesy of Nancy Rasche)

I am also pursuing a Certificate in Acting allowing me to continue my passion for theatre while I attend college. I have been in many shows such as "Little Shop of Horrors" and "12 Angry Jurrors", I was an understudy in Purdue's production of "A Midsummer Night's Dream" and am set to play Mr. Collins in the Fall 2026 semester's production of "Pride and Prejudice".

I also have a passion for 3D printing and am a member and supervisor of the 3D Printing Club at Purdue University. I enjoy printing and finishing helmets and other props from movies, has printed and finished existing as well as his own original models, and has even printed props for theatrical productions. I sell my original 3D models through Instagram with hopes to move sales to Etsy soon.
